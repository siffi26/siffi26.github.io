Siffi Singh

I am an Applied Scientist II at AWS AI, where I build production systems for multilingual conversational AI, speech analytics, and generative AI.

My work spans real-time end-to-end speech translation, multilingual PII redaction, semantic matching across large conversation datasets, trend detection with LLMs, and evaluation frameworks for production AI systems. I care deeply about building AI that is scalable, privacy-preserving, and useful in the real world.

Previously, I worked across Alexa knowledge systems, relation extraction, knowledge graph pipelines, and multilingual QA, and I have also conducted research in face recognition, computer vision, and material property prediction.

I enjoy building products at the intersection of science, engineering, and strategy — turning complex ML systems into capabilities that create real customer value.

Selected Work

Automatic Speech Translation

Built real-time end-to-end speech translation systems with a focus on latency, multilingual expansion, and production readiness for global customer scenarios.

PII Redaction

Developed multilingual privacy-preserving redaction systems using synthetic data, code-switching support, and language-aware tokenization across global languages.

Trend Detection

Built generative AI pipelines for discovering emerging and recurring themes from customer conversations at scale, including evaluation metrics and multilingual support.

Semantic Matching

Improved semantic retrieval across large call and chat datasets with calibrated thresholds, unified modeling, and domain-aware evaluation.

Evaluation and Benchmarks

Contributed to evaluation suites for dialogue summarization, intent embeddings, category discovery, synthetic data generation, and benchmark datasets for relation extraction.
